ABOUT
CIT 8.0

CIT 8.0 is one-of-a-kind opportunity for all aspiring students as we unveil exhilarating challenges of national standard over the realms of basic mathematical aptitude, logical & verbal reasoning, and data interpretation. Not only does CIT implant an idea of various acclaimed aptitude tests like CAT, but our flagship event also never fails to provide a simulation of the overall screening procedure of the hiring companies. This is the most anticipated online event to compete for intellectual glory among a multitude of striving contestants.
